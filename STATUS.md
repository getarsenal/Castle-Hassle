## The siege loop now (the core hook)
1. Walls start **intact** (ring of destructible sections + a timber gate).
2. **Trebuchets bombard** the nearest wall; sections take damage and **crumble into a breach** (archers on a falling section fall with it).
3. You **command your army** — tap to select, drag to set a formation line + facing — and pour through the breach.
4. Fight through the courtyard garrison; break them to take the castle.

## Controls
- One finger = orbit camera (360°); two fingers = pan + pinch zoom.
- Tap unit/card to select; tap field = move; drag = formation line & facing.
- Trebuchets are a unit too — select & reposition the battery to aim at a different wall.
